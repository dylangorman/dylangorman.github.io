HELLO

I am Susannah and I have Rheumatoid Arthritis, I was made Redundant during the 2020 Global Pandemic and started Illustrating with an iPad and Procreate to keep myself occupied while isolating. This reignited an old passion of mine and I started creating pieces of art that evoke all kinds of emotions.
